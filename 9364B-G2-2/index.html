About Inclusion

Inclusion means having an open mindset and giving opportunities to everyone in the community to have an equal chance of living, rights, roles, and getting rid of discrimination on race, disability, gender, medical or any other special needs.


This mindset allows every person in the world to live a happy life without any discrimination despite their differences to each other.Thus, resulting into a much more peaceful society where violence can be easily solved.

Involvement

Elderly Assistance

Camp 7 provides assistance to the elder through the help of the officers that is in charge. By doing this, even elderly people can cope up with the evolving society and their age.

Education

Inclusion in education means that every child or student can learn and be taught equally, giving opportunity for everyone to attend school. Usually, children with mental disorders are often the main topic of inclusion when it comes to education.

Social/Society

An inclusive society does not discriminate the differences of race, gender, class, generation, and geography. It ensures the equality of opportunities and capabilities of every person to have a social institutions that don't discriminate anyone.
